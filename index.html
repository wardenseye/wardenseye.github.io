Features of Warden's Eye
Providing you with everything you'll ever need!
Live Matches

Get real-time information about an ongoing match including the ranks and the champions in use

Summoners' Info

Get the stats of a certain summoner including flex and solo ranks, wins and losses, and most used champions!

Recent Matches

Fetch the stats of a summoner's most recent three matches, and get to know their KDA, positions used, role in the match, and victory status!

Champions' Info

Fetch information regarding a certain champion, find the recommended build, runes, best position and role, and much more!
