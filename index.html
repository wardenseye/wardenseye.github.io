Features
Providing you with the most accurate data possible!
Live Matches

Get real-time information of live games including the summoners' ranks and champions!


Summoners' Info

Get the season's statistics of a certain summoner, including his flex and solo ranks, most used champions and their winrates, and much more!

Recent Matches

Fetch the stats of a summoner's most recent three matches, and get to know their KDA, positions used, role in the match, and victory status!

Champions' Info

Fetch information regarding a certain champion, find the recommended build, runes, best position and role, and much more!
